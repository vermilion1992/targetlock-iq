# TargetLock IQ — Commercial model options

Options for taking TargetLock IQ from prototype to revenue. Figures are **illustrative** for discussion—not offers. Adjust to IMDEX portfolio pricing and regional norms.

---

## Positioning

TargetLock IQ is **decision support software** sold as:

- An **add-on** to IMDEX drilling intelligence (HUB-IQ, SURVEY-IQ, field services), or  
- A **standalone SaaS** module for clients using mixed survey vendors  

Value metric anchors:

- **Metres saved** on deep holes (avoid wedge/branch or redrill)  
- **Target hit rate** improvement on planned intercepts  
- **Supervisor/geologist time** recovered per hole  

---

## Option A — Per-rig subscription

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Unit | Active rig month |
| Typical range (indicative) | USD 800–2,500 / rig / month |
| Includes | App access, updates, standard support |
| Best for | Contractors with clear rig count |

**Pros:** Predictable ARR; aligns with rig-based buying.  
**Cons:** “Inactive rig” disputes; needs usage telemetry for fairness.

---

## Option B — Per-hole / project licence

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Unit | Deep hole package (e.g. >800 m) or project cap |
| Typical range (indicative) | USD 3,000–12,000 / hole |
| Includes | Hole lifecycle, exports, pilot-level support |
| Best for | Exploration juniors, single-hole campaigns |

**Pros:** Easy pilot-to-paid conversion; geology buys per project.  
**Cons:** Revenue lumpiness; hole definition disputes.

---

## Option C — Enterprise site licence

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Unit | Site or country (unlimited rigs) |
| Typical range (indicative) | USD 40,000–150,000 / year |
| Includes | SSO (v2+), SLA, training, integration days |
| Best for | Major miners, long campaigns |

**Pros:** Large deals; IT-friendly.  
**Cons:** Long sales cycle; customization expectations.

---

## Option D — Bundled with IMDEX services

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Unit | Included in directional / surveying service margin |
| Pricing | Embedded in existing contracts |
| Best for | **Ecosystem lock-in**, hardware + data + decision |

**Pros:** No new procurement line; strengthens HUB-IQ narrative.  
**Cons:** Margin visibility; must prove value to avoid commoditization.

---

## Option E — Freemium / CSV-only tier

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Free | CSV import, Simple mode, TXT export (local) |
| Paid | PDF, Advanced, history sync, integrations, multi-hole |

**Pros:** Adoption at juniors; upsell path.  
**Cons:** Support cost on free users; may cannibalize paid.

---

## Recommended hybrid (practical launch)

| Tier | Audience | Model |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Pilot** | 1–2 holes | Fixed fee USD 5–15k or waived for strategic logo |
| **Professional** | Exploration teams | Per-hole or per-rig (A or B) |
| **Enterprise** | Majors | Site licence + integration SOW (C + services D) |

**IMDEX default:** Lead with **Option D** on directional contracts; offer **Option B** for geology-led software procurement.

## Pricing principles

1. **Charge for workflow + integration**, not raw desurvey (commodity).  
2. **PDF handover + approval trail** belong in paid tiers.  
3. **Pilot converts** to per-hole or per-rig within 30 days of close-out.  
4. Avoid unlimited “company-wide” without SSO and support tier.  

---

## Contractual notes

- Software is **decision support**; liability cap and disclaimer in licence (mirror app disclaimer).  
- Client retains responsibility for operational drilling decisions.  
- Data: clarify ownership of surveys and exports (client data).
